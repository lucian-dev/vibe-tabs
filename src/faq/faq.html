Frequently Asked Questions

How do I activate VibeTabs?

To activate VibeTabs, click on the VibeTabs icon in the Chrome toolbar. From there, you can create a new mood or select an existing one to activate. The active mood will change your browser setup according to the tabs and settings youâ€™ve saved.

How do I create a new mood?

Click on the VibeTabs icon in the Chrome toolbar, then click on "Create New Mood". Fill in the necessary details, such as the mood name, page URLs you want to include, and optional background music. Finally, save your new mood.

How can I add a new page/tab to an existing mood?

Right-click on the page you want to add, select "Add to Mood" from the context menu, and choose the desired mood from the list. The page URL will be added to the selected mood without needing to navigate to a separate page.

How do I manage my moods?

Go to the Options page by clicking on the VibeTabs icon and selecting "Options". Here, you can edit, delete, or rearrange your saved moods. You can also add new tabs URLs to moods and change their settings.

How do I activate a mood?

To activate a mood, simply click on the mood name in the extension popup or on the Options page. This will set the selected mood as active, and your browser will adjust to the tabs and settings of that mood.
